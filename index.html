About Us

J & V Hotel & Resort

Established in 2021, J&V Hotel and Resort is owned and managed by Janpac Realty and Development, registered under Securities Exchange Commission (SEC) with business name, Janpac Group of Companies.

Relax in your choice of our guest rooms and suites at J&V Hotel and Resort, each designed to meet the needs of both business and leisure travelers with a range of convenient amenities and services.
